Medieval Grocery Store

A medieval grocery store with some fruit boxes.

  1. The roof cloth is created via nCloth in Maya.
  2. The apples and pears in the boxes are created via MASH library.
  3. The dagger is from the dagger project.
  4. Other props are sculpted and modeled with ZBrush and 3ds Max.

Tools

Modeling: Maya 2019, 3ds Max 2022
Sculpting: ZBrush 2020.1.4
Texturing: Substance Painter 7.1.1
Scene Rendering: Unreal Engine 4.26
Individual Props Rendering: Iray (Substance Painter builtin renderer)
