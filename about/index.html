Tony Kim

Computer Graphics Programmer

Creating new worlds is what I love to do!

The first game that amazed me was Final Fantasy X released in 2001. It was an unbelievable experience, in my age of 9, to explore a vast and a whole new world on TV screen.

The first movie I fell in love was the Matrix. The idea that the whole world is a mere computer program, and that the real world exists outside overwhelmed me. However, how it was visualized, and how much it looked so real and awesome was another shock.

I love building new worlds. The Marvel Universe makes me crazy. And what makes it more attractive is that we can now visualize them as if they really existed. The power of computer graphics. I am a computer graphics programmer as well as a 3D modeler and a look dev artist. I just love what I do.
